ABOUT US

Swati is a professional qualified from India and a talented musician and performer teaching tabla, harmonium and vocals for more than 5 years now in Melbourne. She has been performing at various places and events around Melbourne. Her students include people of different ages, culture and country. She loves to teach kids and encourage parents to let their kids learn music. It helps a lot.

WHY CHOOSE US

The Music School provides young people who possess a passion for music access to quality instruction so they may further explore their interests and continue developing their talents.
